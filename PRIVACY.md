# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-09

TabShelf is a local-first browser extension.

## What data TabShelf uses

- Open tab metadata (title, URL, favicon URL) only when you choose capture or save actions.
- Bookmarks created under a local `TabShelf` bookmarks folder.
- Optional import/export JSON files that you explicitly choose.

## How data is stored

- Data is stored locally using your browser's bookmarks system.
- Legacy local storage data may be migrated once into bookmarks.

## Data sharing

- TabShelf does not transmit your shelf/bookmark data to a remote server.
- TabShelf does not sell personal information.

## Third-party requests

- Favicon images may be requested from site icons or Google favicon service to render bookmark icons in the UI.

## Your control

- You can delete spaces and bookmarks from the extension.
- You can remove all extension data by deleting the `TabShelf` bookmarks folder and uninstalling the extension.

## Contact

For support, use Issues in this repository.
For security vulnerabilities, use the private contact listed in `SECURITY.md`.
